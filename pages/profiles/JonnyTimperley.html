Jonny Timperley – PhD Student

About Jonny

Jonny conducted his MPhil esearch for the SOPWA project, where he assessed the differences in biodiversity and ecosystem functioning of arthropods across oil palm and rainforest systems in Liberia. He is now a PhD student at the University of Liverpool, focusing on developing vibroacoustic monitoring of soil invertebrates in Kenyan savanna.
