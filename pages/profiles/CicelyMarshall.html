Dr Cicely Marshall – Researcher

About Cicely

Dr Cicely Marshall is a botanist, ecologist and conservationist specialising in the west African forest flora. She has a particular interest in traditional ecological knowledge including local plant uses and cultivation systems. As a Research Fellow with King’s College, University of Cambridge, she helped to establish the SOPWA project and published the botanic baseline for the project plots. She now works from the Countryside and Community Research Institute at the University of Gloucestershire, but retains a senior member association with King's College and teaches with Cambridge's Professional and Continuing Education.
