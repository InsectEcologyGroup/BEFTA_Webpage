About Ana

Ana Filipa Palmeirim is a Professor at the Université Libre de Brussels. She studies complex biodiversity responses to human disturbance across the tropics, with a major focus on mammals, amphibians, and reptiles.

Her research investigates the ecological impacts of land-use change, habitat loss, and fragmentation, and more recently the socio-ecological value of forest fragments, including health-related ecosystem services.
