Julia Drewer – Principal Research Scientist

About Julia

Julia is Head of Atmosphere-Biosphere Interactions at the UK Centre for Ecology & Hydrology and a Principal Research Scientist interested in the trace gas fluxes from various ecosystems, including natural and agricultural systems.
