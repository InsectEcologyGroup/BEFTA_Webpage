About Brogan

Brogan L. Pett is a postdoctoral researcher in Evolutionary Biology and Biogeography at the University of Iceland in Reykjavík. His research focuses on understanding spider diversity in tropical Africa, with a special emphasis on the Western Indian Ocean Islands and Central/West Africa.

Brogan joined the SOWPA project three years ago and sorts through our spider samples, describing new species to science along the way.
