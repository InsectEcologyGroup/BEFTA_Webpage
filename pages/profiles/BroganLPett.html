Brogan L. Pett – Postdoctoral Researcher

About Brogan

Brogan L. Pett is a postdoctoral researcher in Evolutionary Biology and Biogeography at the University of Iceland in Reykjavík. Brogan's research focusses on understanding spider diversity in tropical Africa, with a special emphasis on the Western Indian Ocean Islands and Central/ West Africa. Brogan joined the SOPWA project three years ago and sorts through our spider samples, describing new species to science along the way.
